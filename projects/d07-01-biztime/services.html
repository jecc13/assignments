Our Services

Pesticides are one of the biggest causes of the decline of the bee in the United States. We make sure to minimize impact on non-target pests through integrated pest management on every home we treat.

PROTECTING THE BEE - We will NOT treat directly onto flowers or flowering plants. A bee flying from garden to garden will spread these residual pesticides causing harm to none target pests.

PROTECTING OUR FOOD - We do not treat your garden with the same treatments we treat your home with. Your garden is protected by Mother Earth treatments, that are food safe, and good for soil too.

PROTECTING OUR WATER - We will not treat directly into or connected to water sources such as ponds, rivers, or water features. We all drink and use water. Lets keep pesticides out.
